About Mana Panta

Who we are and why we exist

Our Story

Mana Panta began as a small farmer cooperative with the goal of reducing cost and increasing incomes. Over time, we formalized into a trust to reach more farmers and provide organized input support, training and market access.

What we do

  • Distribute quality seeds and recommended fertilizers
  • Provide training on sustainable agriculture practices
  • Assist with micro-finance applications and bookkeeping
  • Link farmers with local and regional buyers

Impact so far

We have reached hundreds of small farms, helped improve yields by up to 20% in pilot groups, and successfully organized weekly buyer-seller meetups that reduce middlemen margins.
